Introduction

At OPTIMA, we offer a technological solution designed to transform aircraft maintenance management. Our platform ensures complete component traceability, facilitates personnel control, meets the highest safety standards, and generates reports quickly and efficiently.

OPTIMA combines a user-friendly interface with a powerful tool, making maintenance management easy to use and highly effective.

This project was launched in 2025 with the goal of modernizing the aeronautical sector in Uruguay, bringing innovation and professional tools to workshops, independent companies, and educational institutions.
